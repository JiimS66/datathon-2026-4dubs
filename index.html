Air for All Good

2025 In-Depth Insights into Air Quality and Livability Across the United States

This project aims to reveal the true picture of air quality across the United States through data visualization and analysis. We begin with a geographical overview of the entire country, capturing the current air quality status of each state and county. We then delve deeper, analyzing regional differences and the causes of core pollutants such as PM2.5 and ozone. Finally, we construct a livability risk assessment model to intuitively identify high-risk areas and areas with a high quality of life.

Let's use data to collectively focus on respiratory health and the future of our environment.
